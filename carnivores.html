Pinguicula gigantea

This tropical Mexican butterword secrets an abundence of sticky mucilage that lures and traps insects. It is one of the only species in its genus to have secretions on both the tops and undersides of its leaves. It puts out bright purple flowers throughout the majority of the year. It is great for catching and trapping fruit flies in the house!
